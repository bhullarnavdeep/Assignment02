SIDHU MOOSE WALA

Biography

Shubhdeep Singh Sidhu (11 June 1993 – 29 May 2022), known professionally as Sidhu Moose Wala Sidhu Moose Wala, was an Indian rapper and singer. He worked predominantly in Punjabi-language music and cinema. Moose Wala is generally regarded to have been one of the greatest Punjabi artists of his generation and to many, amongst the greatest and most controversial Punjabi artists of all time.Moreover, he was considered a key figure in opening the door to mainstream music for Punjabi artists.

Moose Wala was shot dead by unidentified assailants in his car on 29 May 2022 in Jawaharke village of Mansa. According to police, Lawrence Bishnoi's gang initially claimed responsibility for the murder in an unverified Facebook post.
